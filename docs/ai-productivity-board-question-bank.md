# AI Productivity Board Question Bank

Prepared as a Fortune 500-style business intelligence question framework for executive, operational, HR, product, AI operations, security, cost, forecasting, and recommendation dashboards.

## Scope and assumptions

- The dashboard inputs described in the brief are treated as the core telemetry foundation.
- Some questions, especially ROI, compliance, and prediction topics, will be stronger if the platform is enriched with HRIS, finance, audit, and policy metadata.
- Each question is mapped to why it matters, the minimum data fields required, the recommended visualization, the KPI to monitor, and the decision it should enable.

## Coverage summary

- 10 dashboard categories
- 32 subcategories
- 320 business questions
